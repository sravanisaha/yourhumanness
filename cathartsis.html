Cathar(t)sis

Make something with your full attention.

A mindfulness-based creativity workshop. Analog writing and art-making, away from the pull of every screen.

Creativity is what attention does when it has room to breathe. Cathar(t)sis is a held space — pen, paper, paint, presence — where the practice of mindfulness and the practice of making meet.

Each session begins with grounding meditation and moves into guided creative prompts. No artistic experience required. The goal isn't a finished piece. It's the rare, restorative experience of being fully here while making something with your hands.

Who it's for

Individuals seeking creative renewal. Corporate teams looking to unlock real innovation. Retreats wanting a deep, embodied centerpiece.
