Immersive Labs

Where the science of attention meets creativity and connection.

Our signature science-backed, mindfulness-based analog labs designed to reconnect you with your core human qualities; away from digital distractions.

Immersive Lab 01

Cathar(t)sis

A mindfulness-based creativity workshop using analog creative exploration to cultivate attention and authentic human creativity.

When we rely on machines to create everything, our human creativity rusts with disuse.

Cathar(t)sis is a space to reclaim yours. Through mindful analog creative exploration away from screens, participants reconnect with their creative instincts, deepen their attention, and experience the inner joy of slow creation.

Suitable for individuals, corporate teams, leadership offsites, and retreats.

Experience Cathar(t)sis
Immersive Lab 02

The Listening Lab

An immersive lab for cultivating attention in conversation - building deeper human relationships in a distracted world.

Real listening is a rare human skill in the current times. The Listening Lab is a structured, mindfulness-based experience that helps participants slow down, pay genuine attention, and connect with others deeply. The result is stronger relationships, better communication, and a deeper sense of human presence.

Suitable for individuals, corporate teams, leadership offsites, and retreats.
