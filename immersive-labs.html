Immersive Labs

Where attention meets creativity and connection.

Two science-backed, mindfulness-based labs designed to reconnect you with your most essentially human qualities — away from screens, distractions, and noise.

Immersive Lab 01

Cathar(t)sis

A mindfulness-based creativity workshop using analog writing and art-making to cultivate attention and authentic human creativity.

In a world where creativity is increasingly outsourced to machines, Cathar(t)sis is a space to reclaim yours. Through mindful analog writing and art-making — away from screens — participants reconnect with their creative instincts, deepen their attention, and experience the quiet joy of making something by hand.

Suitable for individuals, corporate teams, leadership offsites, and retreats.

Immersive Lab 02

The Listening Lab

An immersive mindfulness lab for cultivating attention in conversation — building deeper human relationships in a distracted world.

Real listening is one of the rarest human skills. The Listening Lab is a structured, mindfulness-based experience that helps participants slow down, pay genuine attention, and connect with others in a way that feels increasingly rare. The result is stronger relationships, better communication, and a deeper sense of human presence.

Suitable for individuals, corporate teams, leadership offsites, and retreats.
