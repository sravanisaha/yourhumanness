For Individuals

If you're seeking deeper focus, cognitive resilience, mental wellbeing and a deeper connection with yourself, our MBSR-inspired mindfulness programs offer a steady, science-backed path.

Our immersive labs, Cathar(t)sis and The Listening Lab, help you reconnect with and explore your creative thoughts and build more meaningful relationships.
