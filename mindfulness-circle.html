Our Mindfulness Circle

Steadying attention and bringing calm-one breath at a time

Structured mindfulness and meditation programs for deepening your practice, and a free weekly community circle that meets every Saturday morning.

Section One

Mindfulness Programs

Our mindfulness and meditation programs are secular, evidence-based, and adapted from the scientific principles of MBSR(Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction). Weaving guided meditation, gentle inquiry and the science of attention, our programs help you cultivate present moment awareness, deep attention, and the resilience to handle the eccentricities of life.

Our sessions are structured to be easily introduced into your daily life, and held in small groups. Whether you’re a busy professional looking to bring a little more calm into your demanding life, or you’re simply looking to lead an intentionally mindful life, our programs are perfect for you.
