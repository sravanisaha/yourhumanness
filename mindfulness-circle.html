Our Mindfulness Circle

Steady your attention and bring in calm ~ one breath at a time

Structured mindfulness and meditation programs for cultivating attention and calm, and a free weekly community circle that meets every Saturday morning.

Section One

Mindfulness Programs

Our mindfulness and meditation group programs are secular, evidence-based, and adapted from the scientific principles of MBSR(Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction).

Weaving guided meditation, gentle inquiry and the science of attention, our programs cultivate present moment awareness, deep attention, and cognitive resilience.

Whether you’re looking at introducing mindfulness into your demanding life, or for reconnecting with yourself, our programs are perfect for you.

Section Two

The Saturday Community Gathering

A free, weekly online community sit for shared silence and to keep a steady meditation practice.

Every Saturday, 10:00–10:45am EST.

We share a safe, secular and quiet space together. Beginners are warmly welcome. Join us!
