In Collaboration with Creative Community Hive

Three creative mindfulness workshops, this July.

A summer series in Oakville and Burlington — combining mindfulness, language, and art across three evenings of creative exploration.

A Creative Collaboration

Your Humanness × Creative Community Hive

This July, Your Humanness is partnering with Creative Community Hive — a creative arts nonprofit based in Oakville — to bring three mindfulness-based creative workshops to local libraries. Each session blends mindful attention with a different creative practice: storytelling, poetry, and art journaling.
